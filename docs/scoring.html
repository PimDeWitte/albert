Multi-Dimensional Scoring

The Albert framework employs a sophisticated scoring system that evaluates gravitational theories across multiple dimensions, combining analytical rigor, numerical accuracy, experimental agreement, and theoretical elegance into a unified score.

Master Scoring Formula

S_total = Σ(w_i × s_i) × P_constraints × B_novelty

Where w_i are component weights, s_i are individual scores, P_constraints is the constraint penalty, and B_novelty is the novelty bonus
