Where:

Test Scenarios

Comprehensive test scenarios covering various physical regimes:

1. Circular Orbit Stability

Tests stable circular orbits at various radii

  • Radii range: 3M to 20M
  • Integration time: 1000M
  • Error tolerance: 10^-10
  • Particles: All massive

2. Perihelion Precession

Mercury-like orbit precession measurement

  • Eccentricity: 0.2056
  • Semi-major axis: 100M
  • Expected shift: 42.98"/century
  • Integration: 100 orbits

3. Light Bending

Photon deflection near massive objects

  • Impact parameter: 2M to 100M
  • Expected angle: 4M/b radians
  • Particle: Photon
  • Accuracy: 0.01%

4. Gravitational Redshift

Frequency shift in gravitational fields

  • Emission radius: 3M to ∞
  • Observer: r = ∞
  • Formula: z = √(g_tt(r_e)/g_tt(∞)) - 1
  • Validation: GPS satellites

5. ISCO Dynamics

Innermost stable circular orbit physics

  • Black hole spin: -1 to 0.998
  • ISCO radius: 1M to 9M
  • Orbital frequency: Ω_ISCO(a)
  • Stability test: Lyapunov exp.

6. Penrose Process

Energy extraction from rotating black holes

  • Ergosphere: r < 2M
  • Max efficiency: 20.7%
  • Spin parameter: a > 0
  • Energy gain: ΔE/E_in
